→ Foundation (Data): 30-40% of project time

**3/**

Before ANY AI project, answer these 3 questions:

1. Does this solve a $100K+ problem?
2. Do we have the data to feed it?
3. Will people actually use it?

Can't say "yes" to all three? Stop. Pick a different use case.

This filter alone saves companies months of wasted budget.
