If you manage rentals, you probably spent this week calling tenants about late rent.

Their responses? "I forgot!" "I thought it was due on the 5th?" "I've been traveling."

Sound familiar? 😅

Here's what we found analyzing 12,000+ units:

67% of late rent happens because tenants simply forgot—not because they can't pay.

It's not you. It's the system.

No reminders until rent is already late. Clunky payment portals. Due dates that don't match paycheck schedules.

The fix? Automated reminders 7, 3, and 1 day before due date. Frictionless mobile payment. Auto-pay options.

One client cut late payments by 65%.
