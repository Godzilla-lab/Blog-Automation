---

**2/**

The Forgotten Payment Problem:

Your tenants juggle 20-30 bills monthly. Rent is just one.

Here's the insane part:

• Phone bill: Reminders 5 days + 3 days before due
• Credit card: Reminders 7 days + 3 days before due
• Rent: ZERO reminders until they're already LATE

You're set up to fail.

---

**3/**

The Payment Friction Problem:

It's 2026. Your tenants pay every bill from bed at 11pm.

But rent? They're expected to:

• Write a check
• Find a stamp
• Visit an office (9-5, when they're working)
• Or navigate a clunky portal

More friction = more late payments. Every time.

---

**4/**

The automation fix that cuts late payments by 65%:

• Smart reminders at 7 days, 3 days, 1 day, day-of
• 60-second mobile payment (or auto-pay)
• Flexible due dates aligned with paychecks
• Automated late follow-up sequences

Result: 12 hours/month → 30 minutes.
