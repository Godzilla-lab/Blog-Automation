Threads Best Practices:
- Length: 300-500 characters optimal
- Tone: Casual, authentic, conversational
- Mobile-first: Short paragraphs, easy to scan
- Use emojis sparingly for visual interest (1-3 max)
- Start with relatable observation or question
- Focus on one clear takeaway

Structure:
1. **Opening**: Relatable hook that feels like talking to a friend
2. **Main Point**: 1-2 key insights from the blog (don't overload)
3. **Closing**: Soft CTA or thought to ponder

Threads is more casual than LinkedIn but more substantive than Twitter.
Think: "Smart friend sharing helpful advice" not "Corporate announcement"

Write in a way that invites comments and saves.
Use line breaks generously for mobile readability.
Include 1-2 relevant hashtags naturally in the text (not at the end).

Keep it authentic and approachable - this platform rewards genuine conversation over polish.
